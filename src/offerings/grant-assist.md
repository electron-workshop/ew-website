Applying for grants is often difficult — not because the work isn't worthy, but because the process rewards those who already know how to play the game. Grant Assist is how the Electron Network levels that.

## What it involves

We bring you and your collaborators together to get clear — on the grant opportunity, your project's fit, and the story you need to tell. Then we work alongside you to sharpen the application.

**Using AI tools to strategise and draft**
We use AI tools to help you think through the opportunity, identify gaps in your case, strengthen your logic, and draft sections of the application. You know the work; we help you articulate it.

**Finding collaborators for a stronger application**
Many grants favour multi-organisation or cross-sector applications. We can help identify collaborators within the Electron Network — or beyond — who complement your project and strengthen the case.

**Identifying auspice and match funding**
Not every applicant is eligible to apply directly. We can help you find an auspice organisation within the network if needed. We can also help identify match funding or in-kind support opportunities that make your application more competitive.

## Who this is for

Community groups, non-profits, open source projects, and ecosystem initiatives that are doing meaningful work and need help navigating the grant landscape — particularly those with limited capacity for the administrative and strategic side of applications.

## How to get started

Reach out via the [Connect page](/connect/) with a brief description of the grant you're looking at and your project. We'll take it from there.
